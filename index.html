About Me

My first computer was a 300MHz IBM with a monitor that sat atop. It came with Windows 98 and new on the market 56k. The world was at my fingertips. I could learn whatever I wanted and I didn't need to leave the house. I soon upgraded it to Windows 2000, updated the graphics cards, sound cards, and even added CD-RW. I taught myself to program with Visual Basic, I made websites with Frontpage, and by the time Windows XP came out I was hacking other computers.

I'm a lover of all things 90s and some things 00s.
